Gotcha

Automatically send NFTs and emails on users' blockchain actions





Why would one need that?

User Retention

Communicate with your users based on what they do with your dApp or other

User Acquisition

Reach out to users that are doing specific stuff onchain

Research

Get insights about how users interact with smart contracts and get notified

How it works


Knowing a user's Ethereum address we can immediately track their interactions with smart contracts you're interested in. Then we filter these events picking only important ones, prepare relevant messages and send notifications. We can notify the user via email, Telegram, Slack or even an NFT. We can notify someone else too!
